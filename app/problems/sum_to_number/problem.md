# Sum Two Numbers

## Problem Statement

Given two integers `num1` and `num2`, return the sum of `num1` and `num2`.

## Input

- Two integers, `num1` and `num2`.

## Output

- The sum of `num1` and `num2`.

## Example

### Example 1

**Input:**
-12
12

**Output:**
0

## Constraints

- `-1000 <= num1, num2 <= 1000`
